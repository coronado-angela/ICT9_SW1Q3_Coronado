!! modern family !!

!! synopsis !!

Modern Family revolves around three different types of families (nuclear, blended, and same-sex) living in the Los Angeles area, who are interrelated through wealthy business owner Jay Pritchett and his children, daughter Claire and son Mitchell.

!! characters biography !!

!! Jason "Jay" Francis Pritchett !!
the patriarch of the Pritchett family. He is the father of Claire, Mitchell, and Joe. He is married to Gloria and is stepfather to Manny. He is a veteran of the Vietnam War and is the founder and former CEO of Pritchett's Closets & Blinds.
!! Gloria Maria Ramirez Pritchett !!
the feisty, fun-loving Colombian daughter of Pilar Ramirez and Fulgencio Ramirez, wife of Jay Pritchett, and the mother of Manny Delgado and Joe Pritchett. She becomes an American citizen in "Patriot Games". She was formerly a hairdresser, a taxi driver, a spokesmodel, a mover, a philosophy professor, a stay-at-home mom and a housewife, but is currently a realtor.
!! Manuel "Manny" Alberto Javier Alejandro Ramirez Delgado !!
is the Colombian-American son of Gloria from her previous marriage to Javier.
!! Fulgencio Joseph "Joe" Ramirez Pritchett !!
the son of Jay and Gloria Pritchett. He was born in the episode "Party Crasher", just after midnight on his half-brother Manny's 14th birthday. While his first name is Fulgencio, Jay says they will never call him by that name as it was only a way to please Gloria's mother, and instead call him by his middle name Joseph/Joe. Joe went to a preschool called The Learnin' Barn.
!! Philip "Phil" Humphrey Dunphy !!
Claire Dunphy's husband and father of their three children, Haley, Alex and Luke. He works as a real estate agent in California.
!! Claire Melinda Dunphy !!
the daughter of Jay and DeDe Pritchett and Mitchell's older sister. She is also the wife of Phil, and the mother of the Haley, Alex and Luke. Claire was a stay-at-home mom until the fifth season when she started to work for her father at Pritchett's Closets & Blinds. She eventually succeeded him as the CEO of the company before quitting and accepting the position of Senior VP of her favorite organizing chain store, Organize ‘Em.
!! Haley Gwendolyn Marshall Dunphy !!
the eldest child of Claire and Phil Dunphy, the older sister of Alex and Luke Dunphy, the wife of Dylan Marshall and the mother of their twins Poppy and George Marshall. Haley has worked multiple jobs since the series began and currently works as a style editor for the wacky Internet lifestyle brand NERP.
!! Alexandra "Alex" Anastasia Dunphy !!
the middle child and second daughter of Claire and Phil Dunphy. Alex is evidently the most intelligent and accomplished of Claire and Phil's children. She is the older sister to her goofy brother, Luke, and the younger sister to her shallow sister, Haley.
!! Lucas "Luke" Philip Dunphy !!
Claire and Phil Dunphy's third and youngest, though tallest child and is the younger brother of Haley and Alex Dunphy.
!! Mitchell "Mitch" Vincent Pritchett !!
Jay and DeDe Pritchett's son, Claire Dunphy's younger brother, Manny Delgado's stepbrother, Joe Pritchett's half-brother, adoptive father of Lily Tucker-Pritchett and Rexford Tucker-Pritchett and Cameron Tucker's husband. He and Cameron eventually get married in "The Wedding (Part 2)" after many tries.
!! Cameron "Cam" Scott Tucker !!
Barb and Merle Tucker’s son, brother of Pam Tucker and two unnamed siblings, the husband of Mitchell Pritchett and adoptive father of Lily Tucker-Pritchett and Rexford Tucker-Pritchett.
!! Lily Elizabeth Tucker-Pritchett !!
Mitchell Pritchett and Cameron Tucker's adopted Vietnamese daughter. She is the older adoptive sister of Rexford Jason Tucker-Pritchett and the co-owner of Larry.
